Let's split up, gang! We've got a real spooky mystery on our hands this week, and we're going to need a lot more than a box of Scooby Snacks to solve it.

Mystery Inc. recently rolled up to a seemingly abandoned web server, but Velma's triage notes show some seriously eerie activity. It looks like a phantom visitor slipped right through the front door, triggered a hidden mechanism, and walked away with a secret package--all while leaving the rest of us staring at a completely normal, boring wall. The creature left a trail of glowing electronic footprints scattered across the system logs, but they're completely jumbled up. We need you to jump into the Mystery Machine, pull out your magnifying glass, and reconstruct exactly how that phantom pulled off the heist before they get away with it for good!
